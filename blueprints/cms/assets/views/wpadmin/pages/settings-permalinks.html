Permalink Settings

WordPress offers you the ability to create a custom URL structure for your permalinks and archives. Custom URL structures can improve the aesthetics, usability, and forward-compatibility of your links.

Common Settings

Select the permalink structure for your website. Including the post name makes links more readable and helps visitors understand the content they're about to open.

?p=123
/archives/123/
/2024/01/01/sample-post/
/2024/01/sample-post/
/sample-post/

Optional

If you like, you may enter custom structures for your category and tag URLs here. For example, using topics as your category base would make your category links like /topics/uncategorized/. If you leave these blank the defaults will be used.
